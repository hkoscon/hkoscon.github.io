HKOSCon Logos and Usage

Logo of HKOSCon is provided for download, please follow the term of use, you should not apply any modification include but not limited to deformation, reformation, recolour and special effect, under any condition.

Trademark Policy

You have the right to use our logo to:

  • Truthfully describe the Hong Kong Open Source Conference (e.g. a Wikipedia page);
  • Accurately report news;
  • Link to the Hong Kong Open Source Conference websites.

If you're not sure, please feel free to ask us: conference@opensource.hk

Standard of logo usage

Background

Please use the standard yellow logo for white or light colour background, and white for dark colour background.
